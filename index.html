Colin Reavis

My name is Colin, and I have survived 25 trips around the sun. Some of my favorite things in life are good food, good company, and good music. As a child, I wanted to be an astronaut, my major in college was psychology, and now I am learning to code. Hopefully this is the field that sticks, because I am quite tired of changing directions. So far, programming seems right up my alley - I think I have the problem-solving mindset everyone says helps, and I love the small boost of dopamine after finally getting something right after an afternoon of working at it. So far, SQL has been my favorite lesson but I can see how important knowledge of C# is. I am consistently surprised by the amount of progress I have made in the past six weeks in this bootcamp, as I can look through my notes and find things I was freaking out about have since become trivial. I hope I can maintain my focus and keep at this until it's my career!
